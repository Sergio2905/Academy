About us

The Academic Advisor

The Academic Advisor is young, progressive and highly professional team of international education agents. Our company was established in August 2014 and is based in Odessa, Ukraine (the third biggest city in the country).

Why us?

We work with the best educational institutions around the world.

The Academic Advisor is not limited to a specific list of educational institutions, we work with both public and private educational institutions around the world. Our team of specialists carries out an individual selection of training programs and educational institutions for each student, thereby helping to determine the best option for further study, which will fully meet the wishes of our client.

Opportunity for scholarships

Many universities offer generous funding programs in the form of scholarships and grants, making free study abroad even more affordable. The Academic Advisor team has already helped more than one student to successfully prepare and apply for a grant or scholarship for study. In 2018, the amount of all scholarships allocated to our students amounted to $83,000.

Admission to programs of any complexity

Our students have been enrolled in some of the most rated universities in the world, and that is grid-grey we know that it is real to enter there. Admission to some training programs can be quite difficult, but thanks to our extensive experience, we know which strategy will be most effective. Do not doubt your abilities, and we will help you in successful admission.

Making a strong package of documents

We know how to correctly present your achievements to the selection committee. The Academic Advisor helps in the preparation of essays, motivational and recommendation letters, a professional resume (CV), as well as a creative portfolio for admission programs to foreign universities related to design, architecture and music. Also, The Academic Advisor helps to translate and legalize all documents, issue a visa package and carry out high-quality preparation for a visa interview.

Successful preparation for the international IELTS exam

We give a 100% guarantee of passing the IELTS exam for the desired score. Our classes are held in mini-groups (4-6 people), which helps students to better work out all aspects and nuances of the exam. This approach, combined with the rich experience and international education of our teachers, gives an excellent result.

Efficiency and quality of our work

Our work is our reputation. We always work efficiently, competently and complete all tasks on time. With us you will not find incorrectly provided information, long responses to your letters or empty promises. Our team will take care of the full organization of the process of admission to the chosen educational institution, the selection of housing (dormitory or apartment rental), visa processing, as well as booking tickets and transfers for a trip to the country of study.
